Top 3 Tourist Cities in Japan

1. Tokyo

Tokyo is the capital and largest city of Japan. It is known for modernity and tradition blending together.

2. Kyoto

Kyoto is famous for its beautiful temples and traditional culture.

3. Osaka

Osaka is known for its food, entertainment, and friendly people.

Why Visit Japan?

  • Rich culture and history
  • Delicious cuisine
  • Efficient transportation
